Quick Start

Get started in 5 minutes

  • Launch a workspace with 2 / 4 / 8 panes and pick your AI crew

  • Send insights through the Feedback Link overlay without losing focus

AI Comparison (Core Pillar 1)

Compare AI responses side-by-side

  • Mix Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, and private AIs in one workspace
  • Load default or saved prompts before every comparison
  • Highlight strengths with filters, tags, and visual cues
  • Snapshot multi-AI results to share with your team

Ranking & Scoring (Core Pillar 2)

Evaluate and score AI responses

  • Lock criteria once so every comparison uses the same math
  • Use the Ranking panel to score responses in table or radar view
  • Save rankings to the AI Command Center for later reference
  • Annotate why the winner won so every teammate understands

Synthesis (Core Pillar 3)

  • Share syntheses through workspace links or quick actions

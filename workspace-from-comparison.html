🎯 How to Compare AI Responses
Step 1: Look at your workspace panes (ChatGPT, Claude, etc.) and copy the response text.
Step 2: Paste each response into its corresponding pane below (Ctrl/Cmd+V).
Step 3: Toggle "🔍 Highlight Differences" to see where AIs agree, disagree, and differ—instantly.
Step 4: Use the Ranking Panel at the bottom to score each response.

📖 Reading the Highlights

Color Legend:

  • Agreement – All AIs say the same (high confidence)
  • Variation – Similar but phrased differently
  • Divergence – Conflicting or missing information
🤔 Why compare AI differences?

Highlight Diff turns comparison into insight. It helps you:

  • Spot contradictions before they affect your decision
  • Identify the most complete answer across all AIs
  • See which models add nuance and which skip details
  • Build confidence where AIs consistently agree

You're not just comparing text—you're comparing reliability.

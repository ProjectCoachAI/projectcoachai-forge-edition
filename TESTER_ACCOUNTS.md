## Registration Process

All testers will follow the same registration flow as new users:

1. **Download the app** from:
   - `projectcoachai.pages.dev` or
   - `projectcoachai.com` (when active)

2. **Install and launch** the ProjectCoach AI Forge Edition app

3. **Register** with:
   - Their full name
   - Email address (to be provided/assigned)
   - Password (they choose)

4. **Automatic Starter Tier**: Upon registration, all testers automatically receive the **Starter (free) tier** - no payment required.

5. **Full Access**: Testers will have full access to all features for testing purposes.

## Tester User IDs (to be assigned)

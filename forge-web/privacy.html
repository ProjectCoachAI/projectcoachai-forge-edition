Our Promise: Your Device, Your Data

Forge runs entirely on your machine. We never see your data.

  • Privacy-first architecture: All processing happens on our secure servers -- nothing is routed through our servers
  • No content storage: Your prompts, AI responses, and analysis results stay on your machine
  • Swiss jurisdiction: Protected by Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (FADP)
  • Desktop application: Comparison, synthesis, and decision tools run locally inside the Electron app
🔒 Zero Data Collection: We don't track, store, or transmit your prompts, AI responses, or analysis content.

How Forge Works

Local Processing: When you compare AI responses in Forge:

  1. You enter prompts in the application
  2. AI responses are displayed in isolated panes within the app
  3. Comparison and synthesis happen locally on your machine
  4. Results are displayed immediately in the application
  5. No data is sent to ProjectCoachAI servers
💻 Local Processing: All comparison, synthesis, and decision-support features run entirely on our secure servers. Your prompt content is never stored or shared.

AI-Assisted Features

Synthesis & Decision Support (Optional): Forge's synthesis engine uses AI assistance to help you compare perspectives and reach decisions:

  • What AI does: Generates synthesis frameworks, comparison summaries, and decision guidance
  • What you control: You choose which AI models to consult, which prompts to send, and which conclusions to draw
  • Compliance: AI assistance supports your analysis -- you own every decision
  • Transparency: All AI-assisted output is clearly labeled
⚠️ Important: AI synthesis is optional. You can use Forge purely as a multi-AI comparison workspace without generating synthesis reports.

Compliance & Security

Xencore Global ensures enterprise-grade security and compliance:

  • Swiss Data Protection: Compliant with Swiss FADP and GDPR
  • Regular security audits: Enterprise-grade security practices
  • Content privacy: Workspace data is stored locally on our secure servers and never transmitted
  • Transparent practices: No hidden data collection or tracking
  • Web application: Built with modern web security standards

What We Don't Do

To be completely transparent:

  • ❌ We don't analyze your AI responses
  • ❌ We don't store your prompts or conversations
  • ❌ We don't track your usage patterns
  • ❌ We don't create derivative content from your data
  • ❌ We don't share your data with third parties
  • ❌ We don't use your data for training or improvement

Your Rights

Under Swiss and EU data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about any data we might have (we don't collect any)
  • Deletion: Delete your local workspace data at any time
  • Transparency: Full disclosure of our data handling practices (this page)
  • Control: Complete control over your workspace and data

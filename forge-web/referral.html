Questions

Frequently asked

What counts as a referral?
+
A referral counts when someone clicks your unique referral link, creates a Forge account, and subscribes to a paid plan (Creator or Pro). Free sign-ups alone don't count toward your tier — it's paid subscribers that matter. This ensures the programme rewards genuine advocates, not just people sharing a link.
How long does the referral cookie last?
+
30 days. If someone clicks your link and signs up within 30 days — even if they don't subscribe immediately — you'll still get credit when they do subscribe, as long as it's within that window.
When do free months get applied?
+
Automatically, within 24 hours of a referred subscriber's payment being confirmed. You'll receive an email notification and the free month appears in your billing cycle immediately. If you're on a free plan, the credit is held and applied when you upgrade.
Can I refer someone who already uses Forge?
+
Referrals only count for new accounts. If someone already has a Forge account and clicks your link, it won't generate a referral credit. The programme is designed to reward bringing new people to the platform.
What is the Legend tier revenue share?
+
Legends (50+ paid referrals) qualify for a revenue participation arrangement — a percentage of net Forge subscription revenue driven through their referral link. This is governed by a formal Revenue Participation Agreement signed with Xencore Global GmbH. It's not equity or shares in the company — it's a contractual revenue share, active while you remain an engaged Forge partner.
Can students participate?
+
Absolutely. Students are some of our most effective referrers — you know your community better than anyone. The referral programme works independently of the student discount. You can refer at your student price and still earn rewards for every paid subscriber you bring in, regardless of whether they're a student or not.
Is there a limit to how much I can earn?
+
No cap. Every paid referral earns a reward. If you refer 100 people, you earn rewards for all 100. The tiers are milestones that unlock better rewards — they don't replace previous rewards. At the Legend tier, there's no ceiling on the revenue share either, as long as you remain an active Forge partner.

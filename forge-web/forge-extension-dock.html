What are the key considerations when expanding a SaaS product into European markets?

✦

Expanding a SaaS product into European markets involves several critical considerations across legal, technical, and business dimensions.

Regulatory compliance is the most significant factor. GDPR applies to any product processing EU residents' personal data — this means data residency decisions, consent management, and Data Processing Agreements with your customers are non-negotiable. You'll also need to consider DSA and DMA obligations depending on your scale.

Data localisation is increasingly expected by enterprise buyers in Germany, France, and the Nordics. Hosting in eu-west or eu-central regions and being able to demonstrate it is often a procurement requirement.

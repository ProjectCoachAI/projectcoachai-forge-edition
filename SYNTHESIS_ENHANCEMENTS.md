# Synthesis Page Enhancements & OpenAI Display

- Mentions "AI assistance (OpenAI, Claude, etc.)"

  - "AI assistance for structure only • You provide all content"

- Button: "🚀 Generate 7 Analysis Templates" (was "Run AI Synthesis")

- Compliance notice: "Templates are AI-assisted. All analysis content is YOURS."

### 6. **Results Section Updated**
- Header: "📝 Your Analysis Workspace" (was "Synthesis Results")

- Export buttons: "Export Templates" (was "Export All")

### 7. **CSS Styling Added**
- Complete styling for compliance banner
- Methodology disclosure styling
- Responsive design for mobile
- Visual hierarchy for compliance notices

    Each response is evaluated comparatively across three dimensions using linguistic analysis heuristics:

    How Forge evaluates perspectives

    Forge uses deterministic, local heuristics to compare response quality. It does not fetch external scoring services and it does not treat any model as inherently better.

    • Structural Clarity: organization, transitions, and coherence signals.
    • Perspective Diversity: variety of framing, terminology breadth, and angle coverage.
    • Evidence Confidence: concrete details, quantified claims, and specific references.

    Decision confidence is derived from alignment and consistency across available perspectives. These are guidance signals designed to support your judgment, not replace it.

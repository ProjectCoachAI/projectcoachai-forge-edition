## 🚀 Getting Started

### How do I get started with Forge?
**1. Select Your AI Tools**  
Choose 2-4 AI tools you want to compare (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity, etc.).

**2. Log In to Each Tool**  
Use your existing credentials for each AI service. Forge opens the official AI websites—you log in normally.

**3. Start Using Forge**  
Switch between Quick Chat (single AI) and Multi-Pane (compare multiple AIs). Your workspace is ready!

**Note:** Your login sessions stay completely local on your machine. Forge never sees or stores your passwords.

---

### How do I connect my AI accounts?
When you open Forge and select an AI tool, it loads the official website (claude.ai, chat.openai.com, etc.) in a secure browser view. Simply log in as you normally would. 

Your session cookies are stored locally on your device—Forge acts like a browser tab. You can manage connected accounts in **Profile → Account Settings → Connected AI Accounts**.

---

### Do I need API keys?
No! Forge uses your regular AI chatbot accounts through their web interfaces. You don't need developer API keys, and you won't pay API costs. Just log in with your normal email/password like you would on ChatGPT or Claude's website.

## 💬 Features & Usage

### What's the difference between Quick Chat and Multi-Pane?
**Quick Chat** — Chat with one AI at a time with instant switching between AIs  
- Best for: Normal conversations, focused work
- All 15+ AI tools available via tabs at top
- Fastest way to use different AIs

**Multi-Pane** — Compare 2-8 AIs side-by-side with the same question  
- Best for: Research, fact-checking, comparing responses
- Send same prompt to all AIs at once
- Auto-ranking and AI synthesis included
- See which AI gives the best answer

Both modes are included in all plans (including Free).

---

### How does "Send to All AIs" work?
In Multi-Pane mode, type your prompt once and click **"Send to All AIs"**. Forge sends the same prompt to every AI in your workspace simultaneously. You'll see all responses side-by-side within seconds.

This is perfect for:
- Comparing AI perspectives
- Fact-checking across multiple sources
- Finding the best answer quickly
- Research and analysis

---

### What is AI Synthesis?
AI Synthesis combines responses from multiple AIs into one ranked summary. Forge analyzes all answers and:
- Ranks them by quality (coherence, creativity, accuracy, overall)
- Shows which AI gave the best response
- Highlights key insights from each

**Free Plan:** 30 syntheses/month  
**Creator Plan:** 100 syntheses/month  
**Professional Plan:** 500 syntheses/month  
**Team Plan:** Unlimited

Note: Running comparisons (Multi-Pane) is always unlimited and free—synthesis is just the auto-ranking feature.

---

### How does auto-ranking work?
Forge uses AI to evaluate responses based on:
- **Coherence** — How well-structured and clear is the answer?
- **Creativity** — How unique or insightful is the response?
- **Accuracy** — How factually correct and reliable is it?
- **Overall** — Combined weighted score

Rankings happen locally on your device. You can view detailed scores and manually override if you prefer a different AI's answer.

## 📝 Prompt Library

### What is the Prompt Library?
The Prompt Library stores all your frequently used prompts for quick reuse. Instead of retyping "Explain quantum physics in simple terms" or "Write a blog post about...", save it once and load it anytime.

**Features:**
- Save unlimited prompts (all plans)
- Search and filter (All, Favorites, Recent, This Week)
- Auto-categorization (Geography, Coding, Writing, Email, etc.)
- Usage tracking (see which prompts you use most)
- Collapsible categories for easy organization

Access: **Profile → Prompt Library** or click **📋 Load Prompt** button

---

### How do I save a prompt?
**Method 1:** When using Multi-Pane's "Send to All AIs" feature, click **⭐ Save Prompt** before sending.

**Method 2:** Go to **Profile → Prompt Library** and manually add a new prompt.

**Method 3:** After asking a question, click the **⋮ More** menu and select "Save to Library"

Saved prompts include:
- The prompt text
- Date created
- Times used
- Last used date
- Custom category (optional)

---

### How do I load a saved prompt?
Click the **📋 Load Prompt** button (top-right or bottom bar), then:
1. Search or browse your saved prompts
2. Filter by: All, Favorites, or Recent
3. Click **Load** on the prompt you want
4. It auto-fills into your input field

You can load prompts in both Quick Chat and Multi-Pane modes.

---

### Can I organize my prompts into categories?
Yes! Prompts are automatically categorized:
- 🌍 Geography & Education
- 💻 Coding & Development
- ✍️ Writing & Content
- 📧 Email Templates
- 📊 Data Analysis
- 🎯 Marketing
- 💼 Business
- 🗂️ Uncategorized

You can also create custom categories in **Prompt Library Settings**.

Categories are collapsible, so even if you have 100+ prompts, your library stays organized and scannable.

---

### What's the difference between "All Prompts" and "Favorites"?
**All Prompts** — Every prompt you've ever saved (auto-deletes after 60 days if not favorited)

**Favorites** — Prompts you've starred (⭐) to keep forever  
- Never auto-delete
- Always accessible
- Quick access via "Favorites" filter

**Tip:** Favorite your most-used prompts so they're always one click away!

---

### Do prompts auto-delete?
**Non-favorited prompts:** Auto-delete after 60 days of no use (Free plan)

**Favorited prompts:** Never delete (all plans)

**Paid plans:** Extend auto-delete to 90 days (Creator), 180 days (Professional), or never (Team)

You'll get a notification before any prompt is deleted. You can also export your entire library anytime.

Go to **Profile → Usage Statistics** to see:

- AI tools used (e.g., "7/15 AI tools — Try more!")

This helps you understand your AI usage patterns and discover which tools you prefer.

In **Profile → Notifications**, toggle:

### How do I manage connected AI accounts?
Go to **Profile → Account Settings → Connected AI Accounts** to:
- See which AIs you're logged into (● Connected / ○ Not Connected)

Example:

This adds an extra security layer to your Forge account (not the individual AI accounts—those have their own 2FA settings).

### Can I change my email or password?
Yes, in **Profile → Account Settings**:

## 🔒 Privacy & Security

### Is my data private and secure?
**Yes.** We take Swiss privacy seriously.

**Your AI conversations:**
- Stay completely local on your machine
- Never transmitted to Forge servers
- Never stored by Forge
- Never accessed by Forge team

**Your Forge data (prompts, settings):**
- Encrypted in transit and at rest
- Stored on Swiss servers (GDPR compliant)
- Never shared with third parties
- You can export or delete anytime

**Your login credentials:**
- Never seen by Forge
- Stored locally by your browser
- We don't manage API keys
- You maintain direct relationship with AI providers

See **Profile → Data & Privacy** for full details.

---

### What data does Forge collect?
**We collect:**
- Account info (email, name, preferences)
- Usage statistics (prompts saved, syntheses used)
- Saved prompts (stored encrypted)
- Billing information (processed by Stripe, not stored by us)

**We never collect:**
- Your AI conversations
- Your AI login passwords
- Your AI API keys
- Content of your chats

---

### Where is my data stored?
- **Servers:** Switzerland (GDPR compliant)
- **AI Sessions:** Local on your device

- **Saved Prompts:** Encrypted Swiss servers

We chose Switzerland for its strong privacy laws and neutrality.

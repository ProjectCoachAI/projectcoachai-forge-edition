Our Promise: Workspace Only

We provide the comparison workspace. You provide the analysis.

  • We never see your data: All processing happens in your browser
  • No server storage: Your prompts and AI responses stay on your device
  • Swiss jurisdiction: Protected by Swiss Federal Act on Data Protection (FADP)
  • Client-side only: Comparison and analysis tools run locally in your browser
🔒 Zero Data Collection: We don't track, store, or transmit your prompts, AI responses, or analysis content.

How It Works

Client-Side Processing: When you compare AI responses:

  1. You enter prompts in your browser
  2. AI responses are displayed in isolated panes (BrowserView)
  3. Comparison happens locally using JavaScript
  4. Results are displayed immediately in your browser
  5. No data is sent to our servers
💻 Local Processing Indicator: When you see "Processing locally... (No data sent)", that's exactly what's happening - everything stays in your browser.

AI-Assisted Features

Template Generation (Optional): Our synthesis framework feature uses AI assistance for template generation only:

  • What AI does: Generates empty templates and frameworks for structure
  • What you do: Fill templates with your analysis, judgments, and conclusions
  • Compliance: AI assistance is limited to structure - you provide all substantive content
  • Transparency: All AI assistance is clearly disclosed with compliance notices
⚠️ Important: AI-assisted template generation is optional. You can use manual templates or create your own analysis frameworks.

Compliance & Security

Xencore Global ensures enterprise-grade security and compliance:

  • Swiss Data Protection: Compliant with Swiss FADP and GDPR
  • Regular security audits: Enterprise-grade security practices
  • Client-side encryption: Workspace data encrypted in browser storage
  • Transparent practices: No hidden data collection or tracking
  • Open source components: Electron framework with security best practices

What We Don't Do

To be completely transparent:

  • ❌ We don't analyze your AI responses
  • ❌ We don't store your prompts or conversations
  • ❌ We don't track your usage patterns
  • ❌ We don't create derivative content from your data
  • ❌ We don't share your data with third parties
  • ❌ We don't use your data for training or improvement

Your Rights

Under Swiss and EU data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about any data we might have (we don't collect any)
  • Deletion: Delete your local workspace data at any time
  • Transparency: Full disclosure of our data handling practices (this page)
  • Control: Complete control over your workspace and data
